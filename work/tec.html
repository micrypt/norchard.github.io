Technology for Education Consortium

TEC is a not-for-profit organization whose mission is to create an effective technology procurement system for schools.

Branding, Illustration, Information Architecture, Visual Design

Branding

TEC needed a mark that stood out in the edtech space - something friendly and modern. The logomark is a symbol of progress through collaboration. Simplicity of form and a bright color palette bring sophistication and fun to the too-often stale edtech market.

Illustration

Introducing the TEC mission began by identifying the shortcomings of the status quo. Illustrations were a playful solution to a gloomy subject matter. Simple compositions depicting curious yet telling scenes provide clarity and delight to the underlying message.

Information Architecture and Visual Design

TEC's website accomplishes two primary objectives: introduce and educate. The fresh look and clean layout provide a solid foundation for delivering a powerful message.
