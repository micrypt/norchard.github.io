Kulen Outreach

A non-profit based in the Phnom Kulen region of Cambodia whose mission is to bring quality education to rural children.

Branding, Information Architecture, Visual Design

Branding

Kulen Outreach needed a logomark that reflected the curiousity and enthusiasm of the kids in Phnom Kulen. The mark pays homage to the Cambodian flag, honoring tradition and heritage, while looking to a future brightened by foundational learning. The color palatte is a nod to the lychee fruit - the namesake of Phnom Kulen. Four custom patterns inspired by Cambodian tapestries reinforce the friendly personality of the Kulen Outreach brand.

Information Architecture and Visual Design

For an organization based in Cambodia, a website is an invaluable communication tool. Kulen Outreach's website was designed to facilitate easy access to information for potential donors and volunteers. High quality photography is featured on a large scale, giving users a sense of the real impact Kulen Outreach is making for the children of Phnom Kulen.
